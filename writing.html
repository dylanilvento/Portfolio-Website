Ward  Business  Plan

Parsing the Games Industry

In an annual survey conducted by the Entertainment Software Association, 22% of people surveyed indicated that games possessing an interesting story or premise influenced them to purchase a video game. This factor, above all others, was the most influential amongst respondents. However, large firms in the video game industry are only interested in producing games that put gameplay first and narrative second. Ward means to change that.
